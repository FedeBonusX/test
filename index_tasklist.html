User Testing Scenarios & Tasks

Scenario 1: First-time User Profile Creation

Imagine you've just downloaded BonusX for the first time. The first step is to create your profile. Assume: 1 citizenship, domicile = residence address, no civil invalidity, main activity is 'Lavoro dipendente' (Employee).

Task 1: Create Personal Profile
Go to the profile page and create your personal profile with the given assumptions.
Task 2: Add Middle Name and Second Citizenship
You forgot to enter your middle name and recently obtained second citizenship. Find where to add your middle name and add a second citizenship.
Task 3: Change Your Job
You've left your job as an employee and are now a freelancer ('Lavoratore autonomo'). Update your employment information.

Scenario 2: Life Changes and Updates

Currently single and living by yourself. Your spouse has no recognized civil invalidity.

Task 1: Update Living Situation
You are single and live by yourself. Where would you add this information in your profile page?
Task 2: You are now married!
You have gotten married. Update your status to reflect that you are now married ('Coniuge o unito/a civilmente').
Task 3: Update Spouse's Information
Your spouse has started a new job as an employee ('Lavoro dipendente'). Find where to update your spouse's profile and make that change.
