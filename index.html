User Testing Scenarios & Tasks

Scenario 1

Imagine you've just downloaded BonusX for the first time. The first step is to create your profile.

For this scenario, assume the following about yourself:

  • You have 1 citizenship.
  • Your domicile address is the same as your residence address.
  • You do not have any recognized civil invalidity.
  • Your main activity is 'Lavoro dipendente' (Employee).
Task 1: Please go through the process of creating your personal profile.
Go to the profile page.
Task 2: Add a Middle Name and a second Citizenship
Oh! You just realized that you forgot to enter your middle name and the recently second citizenship you have obtained, when you signed up. Please find where you would add your middle name and add a second citizenship and update them.
Task 3: Change Your Job
Your professional life has changed. You've left your job as an employee and are now a freelancer ('Lavoratore autonomo'). Please update your employment information.
